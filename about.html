About Me

A powerful marketer, growth hacker and effectual brand leader with a proven ability to develop new opportunities and customer networks, I am a ‘big ideas’ product marketer, who has been able to combine business acumen with creativity consistently throughout the career. My unique perspective reflect my unusually wide experience, spanning the creation of new brands and new product, to lead the brand rationalizations and, uniquely, also has been deeply involved with management, e-commerce, digital media, crowdfunding and online shopping since the earliest days of the Internet and social media.


My focus is on marketing and product development as brand reputation management, which includes creating your product brand identity, telling your brand story; sharing, building and managing your products in social community.

My experience fulfilling directives of marketing, branding and selling, history of interacting with individuals of all capacities, and ability to produce high-quality business supports on a timely basis could provide much value and benefit to your company.
