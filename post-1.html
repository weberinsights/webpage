Storytelling

Weber has been lucky enough to develop over 50 brands or products while enjoying the stability of working at just few companies. That's at the consumer electronics fields in about round the Earth trips for every year to promote the products and participating trade fairs. Seasoned? Yes. Not quite a product marketer, but truly a growth hacker. I’m a blend of both.


“Chasing the wind” is fun, but creative collaboration and results are what fuel me.

Absolutely, I can brainstorm concepts and fine-toning all day. But teamwork makes for awesome sauce! Adapting tone and tactics for so many audiences, brands, and channels may make me a speedy, but the possibilities are endless and there’s always something new to learn. Call me crazy, but I love meetings, new ideas, marketing, and social media.

Customer is the Queen.

Customer service comes first, particularly on messaging, entertaining guests at business atmosphere embedded that value in me. It begins with proactive research and listening. But the voice of the client’s ego also benefits brands if they make such insight into action. Key Account Network which I generate, business leads and profits such as LF, Sears, Best buy, Tesco, Dixons, Metro Group, El Corte, HAMA, Vivanco, Skymaster, Aeropostale, TOYS”R”US and more.

But Weber is not just about business. I am a football lover, proud tech geek, and a ridiculously fashion chaser. I watch the House of Cards, but sometimes the Running Man; listen to Hotel California, and I like making people laugh. If it’s not fun, it’s not worth doing, right?
